About My Self:

Hello, I am Bii Ivyn Ketra, a dedicated Nutrition and Dietetics Consultant passionate about promoting health and well-being through evidence-based nutritional guidance. I pursued my Certificate in Nutrition and Dietetics at Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC) Thika, completing my studies in 2022 and graduating in 2024. During my training, I gained hands-on experience through my attachment at Narok Referral Hospital, where I worked closely with healthcare professionals to provide dietary support to patients. I am currently upgrading my qualifications to a Diploma in Nutrition and Dietetics, further expanding my expertise in personalized meal planning, medical nutrition therapy, and wellness coaching. My goal is to empower individuals with practical and science-backed nutritional solutions to achieve a healthier lifestyle.
